Start Your Own Clothing Brand

Modern Uniform was established in 2015 in jeddah, Saudi Arabia by its CEO, Mr. Salem Abdullah Barayan. Its first headquarters and factory was located inindustrial City wherein production and manufacturing of uniform was its major business. Today, Modern Uniform is considered as the pioneering company when it comes to business uniforms manufacturing wherein it boasts of having the best clients from the top companies in the Kingdom. We have a wide range of products for your clothing line, with the branding options of screen printing, embroidery, sublimation and digital (DTG) printing; patterns and styles available in men, women and kids sizes.
