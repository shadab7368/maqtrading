Who We Are ?

MAQMHA Trading – Supplier of Uniforms, Corporate Gifts & Promotional Items

Our uniforms are designed with You in Mind. Uniforms are a mark of respect for the company’s staff. People are the heart of any business and a well-designed uniform boosts the performance and confidence of staff. It gives the worker a sense of pride, responsibility and professionalism. Consumers are able to make a near instant connection to a company’s corporate identity through corporate clothing. As a company’s brand identity gains awareness, consumer connections will become instantaneous. Consumers will connect a brand identity to a specific company through visual branding elements of design, color, symbols and logos. At MAQG Trading we work with our clients to develop a custom program that fits your requirement and budget. A dedicated service representative will be responsible for ensuring you’re always 100% satisfied with our service. With MAQG you will never be asked to purchase based solely on information in a catalogue. Instead, you and your employees will always have the opportunity to examine garment samples before you decide, allowing you to touch and feel the fabric as well as study detailing and make sure it’s right for you. We work closely with our clients in order to design and capture the ideal corporate uniform for their image
